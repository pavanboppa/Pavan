AUSSIE TACOS

The freshness of our ingredients and attention to detail, mark our commitment to Old World New York-style pizza. Our dough is aged, brought to the proper temperature, and hand-tossed to create the perfect bake.
When the ingredients and the art of baking in our 1,000 degree coal fired oven are joined together, we create the most delicious flavors on a thin crust and char them to perfection for your enjoyment.
Our custom made oven is one of the most expensive pizza ovens in the world. It requires a 2 hour pre-lighting process and hourly monitoring in order to provide you with the best pizza imaginable!
